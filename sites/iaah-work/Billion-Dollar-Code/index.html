Billion Dollar Code

A Netflix original series. The plot follows two interconnected timelines. In Berlin of the 1990s a group of young hackers and art students had developed a planet browser in 1991 which was used by Joachim Sauter to get funding from Deutsche Telekom in 1993 to start the successful development of Terravision.

In 2014 a patent infringement dispute against Google, alleging that TerraVision was used to develop Google Earth. The series dramatized the real lawsuit which lasted until 2017.
