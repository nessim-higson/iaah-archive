Love Letters from New Orleans iPad App

The iPad app is now available with an upcoming iPhone version right on it's heels. The app is more than most expect with complete animation and sound design for each spread and haiku. It's the same book, but different. This would not have been made possible without the help of Epilogue ​( Justin Fontenot, Ron Domingue, and Marc Reviere ) have been tremendous in both helping bring this digital version to life but in being a great partner. Also big thanks to Joe Fleming, and Joe Johnson for the wonderful attention to animation and sound design and composing. 

​The app is FREE for a limited time and can be downloaded here.
